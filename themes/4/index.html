Grassroots Organizing and Mutual Aid

From autonomous direct actions to sheltering and feeding thousands of protestors to re-building communities in the wake of superstorm Sandy, Occupy was a movement that relied upon non-hierarchical, people-led (not institutional-, organizational-, or non-profit-led), collective action.
