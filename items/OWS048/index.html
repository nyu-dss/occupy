Laborers Local 108 protest sign

Label
Laborers Local 108 protest sign
Creator
Laborers Local Union 108
Object Type
Signs
Description
Local 108 was just one of many union locals present at Occupy Wall Street. OWS's Labor Outreach Committee helped build connections with NYC labor, leading to greater working class participation in the protests. In this protest sign that read "Wall Street May Have Money and Influence BUT The People Got the Power!" and "LiUNA," Laborers Local 108 show their solidarity with Occupy.
Image Description
This Laborers Local 108 sign is commercially printed on an orange corrugated plastic. In black text it says "Wall Street May Have Money and Influence But.. The People Got the Power!" The People Got the Power! is italicized. Below this phrase is "LiUNA!" in white, followed by "Laborers Local 108" in black.
Citation
