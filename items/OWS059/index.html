Object Type
Organizing Documents
Description
Collective Agreement for Occupy's Office Space at 50 Broadway, the United Federation of Teacher's headquarters in lower Manhattan. The agreement was written by the Occupied Office Coordinator Team and it lists a few guidelines for their indoor space. Using the logo of the television show The Office, the document asks organizers to "respect each other and our hosts/allies at the UFT." It tells activists that the space cannot be used for sleeping or personal storage, and also warns that there is zero tolerance for violence, drugs, and abuse of shared property. Violating these rules would result in a 30-day suspension from the building, and an eventual review of an individual's standing with the office.
