Safer Spaces: Rapists Fuck Off

Label
Safer Spaces: Rapists Fuck Off
Creator
Unknown; Occupy Wall Street Archives Working Group
Object Type
Signs
Description
Carboard sign that reads, "Safer Spaces: Rapists Fuck Off". Includes a sticky note, "You can't live without us' - the govt." Sexual assaults were reported at Zucotti and other camps in October and November. Organizers attempted to confront this issue during the final weeks of the occupation.
