MLK Vigil

Label
MLK Vigil
Creator
J15 Global; Occupy Wall Street Archives Working Group
Object Type
Flyers and Handouts
Description
A flyer for a candlelight vigil in honor of Dr. Martin Luther King that was held on January 15, 2012. The event began with a march that began at St. John the Devine and concluded at Riverside Church where the main program was held. Figures such as Patti Smith, Yoko Ono, and Steve Earle spoke and performed at the event.
