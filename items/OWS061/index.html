Brownsville Stop + Frisk action protest sign

Label
Brownsville Stop + Frisk action protest sign
Creator
Unknown; Occupy Wall Street Archives Working Group
Object Type
Signs
Description
This sign promotes a "Stop Stop + Frisk," action that took place in Brownsville, Brooklyn on November 1, 2011. During this action, protesters marched from the Tilden housing projects to the NYPD’s 73rd and then 23rd precincts.
Image Description
An orange poster board sign says “Stop Stop and Frisk" in black marker. Below this, the sign says "Tuesday, Nov, 1 at 4pm Brownsville Brooklyn." At the bottom of the sign, a web address is outlined.
Citation
Sign promoting Stop Stop and Frisk rally, 2011; TAM.630 Occupy Wall Street Archives Working Group Records; box 18; Tamiment Library/Robert F. Wagner Labor Archives, New York University
