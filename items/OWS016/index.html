Image Description
A blue flyer with black font invites participants for a Day of Trans Awareness in advance of the International Trans Day of Remembrance. The flyer provides dates and times (Sunday, November 13th 4-6 pm), includes a Facebook link and QR Code, and includes the logo created by Queering Occupy. This logo incorprates a transgender symbol as the "O" in Occupy and uses an inverted triangle in the place of the "U" in "Occupy."
