OWS Structure Proposal

Label
OWS Structure Proposal
Creator
OWS Structure Working Group
Object Type
Flyers and Handouts & Handouts
Description
In lieu of Occupy's struggles to meet the "day-to-day operational needs of working groups and caucuses," the OWS Structure Group produced this proposal for future organizing during the fall of 2011. The "living document" sought to address logistical challenges without altering OWS non-hiearchical nature. Organizers identified challenges such as access, transparency, functionality, and decision making as part of a broader effort to maintain a leadersless structure during the occupation.
