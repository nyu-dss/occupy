Description
Carboard sign that reads "Restore American Airlines Flight Attendants Wages." Flight attendants took a 35% pay cut in order to save jobs in the wake of 9/11. American Airlines told their workers that they would increase their retirment and health benefits, but did not follow through on their promises.
