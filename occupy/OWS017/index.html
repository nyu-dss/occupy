Description
Email sent through Fast Mail message service on 10/14/11 from Anthony and Carol Bartleson who sent a camping pad and sleeping bag to assist those at Zucotti. They responded to an email sent by OWS that asked for donatons for those camping at Zucotti. Aside from sleeping bags, blankets, and tarps, the organizers also requested materials for their street medic team including duct tape, cold medicine, and baby wipes. Personal email address has been blocked out.
