Description
Cardboard sign that features a Guy Fawks Mask. Reads "Can't Stop, Won't Stop." The mask became the main logo for "Anonymous," a well-known hacker group that supported Occupy Wall Street and WikiLeaks, among other issues in the early-2010s.
